Synapspeak is a low cost, fast and wearable AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) device that converts a paralysed persons Eye blinks into audible speech.


Synaspeak is cheaper than existing consumer AAC devices like Eye-Speak and Dynavox that costs upto $15,000 (Rs. 9 Lakh +) on the other hand Synaspeak costs $150 max. (Rs. 10,000 max)
